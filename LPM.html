Lite-Predictive Maintence

This programme helps to utilize IoT Devices and Predictive methodology to help companies that handle a large fleet of vehicles to fully utilize their vehicle at the same time reducing the cost of maintaining them by alerting stakeholders when they need of maintaining the vehicle

For this week our partner and I started assembling the hardware to the Raspberry PI and while doing so, we accidentally burned our temperature sensor. Week 10

For this week we decided to add on an LCD screen to keep track of the temperature collected without using the screen. As we had to bring the Raspberry Pi, to the motorcycle to collect the temperature. Upon Deciding on where to collect the temperature we decided to place the sensor below the suspension, near the engine. But however didn’t provide a sensor that is capable to collect a bike’s engine temperature as it was constructed with plastic. Therefore we had to improvise by collecting data from the room.
